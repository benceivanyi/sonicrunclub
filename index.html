A London–based run club for cultural creators
SONIC(LDN) holds biweekly runs, and monthly meet–ups. Our members collectively curate routes and experiences tailed to weather, seasons, and relevant cultural events. All running abilities are welcome.

How to join
The club has an object–linked membership structure. To join, you’ll need to get an item from the latest seasonal object drop.

Each object holder gets to be a member, participate in the activities, planning and governance of the club. DROP 1 ↗ is a t–shirt, your perfect summer companion.
